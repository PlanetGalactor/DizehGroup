Our Story

Founded by Ali Ghanbari

With a Master's degree in Civil Engineering and over a decade of international building experience, founder Ali Ghanbari established Dizeh Group on the pillars of engineering precision and architectural artistry. For more than 10 years, the firm has been bringing sophisticated residential visions to life across Toronto and the Greater Toronto Area.

Design Philosophy

Minimalist Elegance & Natural Materials

Our approach harmonizes Modern, Transitional, and Traditional architecture. We believe in minimalist layouts that emphasize light, volume, and raw natural materials—such as premium limestone, rich oak wood, and crafted metals. Each project is designed to be a quiet statement of luxury, blending seamlessly with its surroundings while providing an unmatched sensory experience.

"Luxury is not about excess. It is the absolute refinement of form, light, and material integrity."
— Ali Ghanbari, Founder

Architectural & Construction Services

01

Custom Home Build & Design

A seamless, turn-key journey from conceptual design to structural completion. We handle zoning, permits, architectural blueprints, and structural engineering with meticulous execution to create your bespoke estate.
